Living in the same town of Teramo (Abruzzo, Italy), being myself a passionate enthusiasts of space, it was inevitable that the author of this site and Italo Rodomonti met. Grateful for seeing his works published online, since a time when the Internet itself was frontier land and a meeting place for a few esteemer, Rodomonti donated some of his works to the webmaster. These works are now available for expositions and galleries.
